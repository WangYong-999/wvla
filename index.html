WVLA

From Whole-Body Control to Vision Language Action Model

Yong Wang1, Ran Cheng2, Kecheng Zheng2, Hua Chen1, Chao Xu1, Shengze Cai1

Method

The proposed approach includes: (a) designing a wearable exoskeleton to control the whole-body motion of a humanoid robot; (b) preserving task-related language descriptions, states, language, and visual information; and (c) designing a visual-language-action model, trained on the acquired dataset, and using the preserved weights to receive robot information in real time for automated whole-body task inference.
